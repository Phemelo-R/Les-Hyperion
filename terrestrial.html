Terrestrial analyses

This section is an introduction to Python-based GIS through a sequence of small, practical notebooks that use freely available spatial data for South Africa. The materials move from simple basemaps and administrative boundaries to land cover, vegetation, wildfire history, elevation, biodiversity records and basic species distribution modelling. Each card links to a rendered notebook that explains the data sources, the code used, and the ecological reasoning behind each map.
